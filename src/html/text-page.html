Conditions

1. QUALITY AND COMPLETENESS

1.1. Quality and completeness of the goods supplied under this Agreement shall comply with TS-3390-001-82126676-2011, as well as other regulatory and technical documents of the manufacturer. The goods shall be new and not previously used. All shipping documents shall be supplied with the goods. Besides, the shipping documents shall include Certificate of Origin of Goods in accordance with the general form issued by SOEX NEVA, LLC that is an integral part of supply. In case of failure to receive any document the BUYER shall notify the SELLER in written within 3 (three) business days from the date of the Goods receipt. Within 5 (five) business days from the date of the written notification receipt from the BUYER, the SELLER shall provide the BUYER with all missing documents.

1.2. The warranty period for the goods supplied under this Agreement shall be 12 (twelve) months from the date of the goods provision by the SELLER to the BUYER, unless otherwise stated by the additional provisions of this Agreement.

1.3. In case of provision the BUYER with the low-quality goods under this Agreement due to the SELLER’S fault (this fact shall be confirmed by the bilateral certificate of by the order specified in Clauses 2.1. and 2.2. of the Agreement, the BUYER at its own discretion is entitled to demand from the SELLER the following:

proportional decrease of the goods purchase price;

free-of-charge defects elimination of the goods within 30 calendar days, however, not less than the period specified for supply for these type of goods upon receipt of the poor quality goods certificate or conclusion of the independent expert organization by the SELLER

2. ACCEPTANCE OF GOODS

2.1. Acceptance of the Goods from the forwarding companies shall be carried out by means of the goods inspection for compliance with the information specified in the accompanying (forwarding) documents in accordance with the current legislation of the Russian Federation and the international law. In all cases when during the Goods receipt from the forwarding companies the goods defects and/or damages are detected, as well as deviation of the name and weight and/or number of colli from the forwarding (accompanying) documents and in any other cases envisaged by the transportation regulations, the BUYER (Consignee) shall demand a commercial report from the forwarding companies.

2.2. In case during the Goods acceptance any deviations of the supplied Goods with regards to its quality are detected, the BUYER/Consignee shall suspend such acceptance and call the SELLER’S representative in written to draw up a bilateral Certificate. In case the SELLER or its representative rejects to take part or fails to appear during the goods acceptance within 10 (ten) business days from the date of the written notification receipt (not considering travel time), the BUYER (Consignee) shall accept the Goods with the Chamber of Trade and Industry representative or any independent expert organization having the relevant authorities. A bilateral Certificate, Certificate of the Chamber of Trade and Industry or a Conclusion of the independent expert organization shall be drawn up on the basis of the goods acceptance results regarding the goods / batch /non-compliance with the provisions of the Agreement or any forwarding documents, containing the grounds of such non-compliance and possible perpetrators, thereby such a conclusion shall be final and binding for both Parties. The Buyer shall pay for the independent expertise services, thereby, the Seller shall compensate all charges to the Buyer related to the independent expertise execution in case the results of such expertise envisage the goods supply with the terms violation with regards to its quantity and quality due to the Seller’s fault.

3. CLAIMS AND ARBITRATION

3.1. All disputes and controversies arising from this Agreement or in relation with it shall be settled by settled in accordance with the complaint procedure.

3.2. All claims related to the Goods quantity and quality shall be provided within 15 /fifteen/ days from the date of the Goods receipt by the consignee from the forwarder with the following documents provision:

  • the certificates of the Chamber of Trade and Industry or any independent expert organization.
  • copies of the consignment notes and forwarding documents.

3.3. In case of the goods quality non-compliance with the provisions of this Agreement, the BUYER is entitled to act in accordance with the provisions of Cl. 1.3 of this Agreement.

3.4. In case of the full or partial rejection to satisfy the claim or failure to receive the relevant answer within 30 /thirty/ calendar days from the date of the claim provision by any Party, the other Party is entitled to appeal to the Arbitration court at the Seller’s domicile.

4. PENALTIES

4.1. In case of the delivery terms violation the SELLER shall be liable in the amount of 0.1 % from the price of non-delivered goods for each day of delay, however, not exceeding 10% from the price of the delayed Goods.

4.2. In case of delayed payment for the Goods the BUYER shall be liable in the amount of 0,1% from the owing amount for each day of delay.

4.3. Besides the liability specified in Cl. 4.2. of this Agreement, in case of the payment delay for the Goods exceeding 5 (five) calendar days, the Supplier is entitled to demand a penalty from the Buyer in the amount of 5 000 (five thousand) USD.

5. FORCE MAJEURE

5.1. The Parties shall be released from the liability for the full or partial non-fulfillment of their obligations under this Agreement, if such non-fulfillment resulted from a force-majeure event, in particular: fire, flood, earthquake or military actions, in case such events directly affected the obligations fulfillment under this Agreement. In this case the period of the obligations fulfillment shall be postponed pro rata to the period during which such force-majeure circumstances or their consequences remain in force.

5.2. A Party unable to fulfill its obligations under this Agreement shall immediately notify the other Party in written, however, no later than 3 days upon the force-majeure circumstances occurrence.

5.3. A proper evidence of the force-majeure circumstances shall be the certificates issued by the Chamber of Trade and Industry of the contracting Parties or by any other authorized state bodies.

6. PAYMENT TERMS (OPTIONAL)

6.1. The date of payment shall be the date when the funds are credited to the account of the Seller

6.2. Under the present contract is not accrued and not paid interest on the amount of debt for the period of utilization of the funds provided for in Article 317.1 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation.

7. OTHER PROVISIONS

7.1. The regulations of this Agreement shall cover all legal relations of the Parties and shall be in force upon the Agreement signing in a hard copy by both Parties until complete fulfillment of the obligations under this Agreement by both Parties.

7.2. All amendments and alterations to this Agreement in a hard copy, as well as all relevant Annexes drawn up in written and signed by both Parties shall be an integral part of this Agreement (in a hard copy).

7.3. Neither party may assign the rights and obligations under this Agreement to any third parties without the written consent of the other Party.

7.4. All relations of the Parties not specified in this Agreement shall be settled in accordance with the current legislation of the Russian Federation.
